Cliffs of Moher

For day 2, we got on the bus and left Galway immediately, and started the drive over to the cliffs of Moher. On the way, we got to stop at a really cool castle that was by the sea, and the weather there was just perfect; it sort of felt like a studio Ghibli movie. We eventually got to the cliffs, which were so beautiful, but I found them quite scary. It seemed like the risk of death was not insignificant there. Nonetheless, I walked a large portion of the cliffs and did my best to really take them in. After that, it was just a long drive back to Dublin.
